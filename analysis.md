### LI — Li Auto Inc. — Conviction: LOW (Value Trap)

**Setup:** 51.5% off 52w high, P/B 1.47 (near book), D/E 24%, and a cash hoard (~101bn CNY ≈ $14bn USD) against a $15.8bn USD market cap — on paper this looks like a net-cash deep value play. Trajectory is flat with only one day of tracking data (both watchlist observations are 2026-05-28); no confirmed multi-week accumulation to lean on.

**Cyclical or structural:** STRUCTURAL HEADWIND. The China EV price war is structural overcapacity, not a cyclical inventory cycle. UBS calls it years-long. BYD's net profit dropped 20% and it responded by *expanding* capacity, not cutting it — there is zero supply discipline in this sector. Li Auto's vehicle margin already compressed from 19.7% to 16.8% YoY in Q4 2025, and EV/EBITDA at -12.1 means EBITDA is negative. Cash burns in a deteriorating-margin environment eat balance sheets.

**Key risk:** The price war does not end. Li Auto's 20% volume growth target for 2026 follows an -18.8% drop in 2025 deliveries. Hitting that target requires taking share from BYD and peers who are pricing aggressively to survive. Even if volumes recover, margins may not — and that is how a cheap P/B becomes a fair P/B that stays cheap. Analyst consensus is cutting (Morgan Stanley, Goldman $19 PT, JPMorgan $15.50 PT — essentially current price).

**Catalyst to watch:** Q2 2026 earnings + monthly delivery figures for June (end-June report). The $1bn buyback announced March 2026 is genuine management conviction, and the new Li L9 cycle launching this year is the volume lever. If June deliveries show >15% YoY growth AND vehicle margin holds above 16%, the setup becomes revisitable. If margins slide further, this is a falling knife.

**Action: PASS**

Reasoning: No estimate upgrade cycle (estimates being cut), no supply discipline (structural overcapacity), no sector inventory drawdown analogue, negative EBITDA, trajectory data is a single day. The net cash balance sheet is the only real bull case, and it requires the business to not burn through it — uncertain at -12.1x EV/EBITDA. Miss a buy here, it costs nothing. Buy a margin-compressing Chinese ADR in an overcapacity sector and hold through further cuts — that costs real money.
